Classic Margherita Pizza

Imported from Naples notes. Forked twice. Last cooked by @rj on May 22.

Spoon thisForkSave
Ingredients
  • Pizza dough1 ball
  • San Marzano tomatoes1 cup
  • Fresh mozzarella6 oz
  • Basil leaves12
  • Olive oil2 tbsp
  1. 01

    Heat the stone

    Put the stone on the highest rack and give the oven a full 45 minutes at 550F. The crust needs stored heat, not just hot air.

  2. 02

    Dress lightly

    Crush tomatoes by hand, tear the mozzarella, and keep the center sparse so the dough does not steam.

  3. 03

    Finish with basil

    Slide, blister, and add basil after the bake so it perfumes the pizza without burning.
